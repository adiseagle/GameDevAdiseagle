  • Car Simulation Project
    • a personal project of mine exploring physics and geometry

    the technical highlight is the suspension physic model that I made from the ground up, it closely mimics real life car suspension behaviour based on my theory.

    one of the suspension is the Macpherson Strut type. it's calculated using vector math extensively with it only need a position and a normal plane as input. it achieves the inverse kinematics through the use of itteration and clever uses of trigonometry.

    this is a Double Wishbone type. it's calculated similar to the previous type but with an extra linkage. but that's not all

    this system also supports anti rollbar and it acts as a physical bar that get twisted on 2 ends much like the real one.
